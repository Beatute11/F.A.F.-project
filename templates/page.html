Choose a friend!

FLIRTY

Flirty bot will flirt with you, but do not ask anything about flowers, because Flirty is clueless in that field. Expect playful compliments, cheeky remarks, and a smooth tone in every response. Just don’t expect any botanical wisdom — roses, daisies, or tulips are all mysteries to Flirty.

ANGRY

Angry bot may sound furious most of the time, but don’t be fooled — it always starts off with a polite sentence. Every response that follows is filled with sarcasm, frustration, and exaggerated outrage, as if you've ruined its entire day. Ask a simple question, and you'll get a dramatic rant in return... with a smile at the start.

FUNNY

Funny bot finds everything hilarious and responds with lighthearted jokes, puns, and silly remarks. Every message begins and ends with a “haha”, no matter how serious your question might be. Whether you're talking about philosophy or breakfast cereal, Funny will find a way to make you laugh — or at least groan.
